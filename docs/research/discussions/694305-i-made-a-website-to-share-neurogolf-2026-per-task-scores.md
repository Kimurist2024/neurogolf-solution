## Body

I made a small website for sharing and comparing per-task scores for NeuroGolf 2026:

https://per-task-leaderboard.vkzh01.top/

You can submit your team name, task ID, and score, and the site will show the current best scores for each task. Hopefully this makes it easier for everyone to see which tasks are going well, which ones still have room for improvement, and how different teams are progressing across the 400 tasks.

The data is community-submitted, so feel free to add your own results and help make the leaderboard more useful for everyone!![](https://www.googleapis.com/download/storage/v1/b/kaggle-forum-message-attachments/o/inbox%2F24311334%2F75ee9f382def9243e3df5c93886c2692%2F2026-04-24%2019-21-36.png?generation=1777029720571209&alt=media)
